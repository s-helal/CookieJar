Ocular Vision Test

Take an at-home eye exam yourself if you can't make it to the optometrist during the pandemic or just want to see how good your vision is.

There are two modes for testing your vision based on your needs: We have a mode for audio-reponse users and for nonverbal/deaf users.

Instructions to use our test:

When shown a line of letters in the alphabet:

- Audio response users will speak the letter which they see on the screen

- Non-verbal/deaf users can show their webcam the American Sign Language hand gesture that corresponds to the letter they see on screen.

Note: Please make sure your webcam/audio settings are enabled before taking your vision test.

OVT's score predictor is not a valid medical diagnosis and you should refer to your primary care physician or an optometrist for a licensed score. Find An Optometrist
