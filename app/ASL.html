ASL-Vision-Tester

Welcome to the Ocular Vision Acuity Test for ASL Users

Before you begin, please make sure your webcam is on and stand 3 ft away from the screen for a more accurate vision score.

Place your device on a level surface where the chart on the screen is directly in your line of vision.

Close one eye and cover it with your palm. Your other eye will be tested for visual acuity.

When you start the test, you must slowly sign the letters that you see in clear view of the webcam, as in the example image below:

Once you have finished testing that eye, you may switch eyes and repeat the test again for the other eye.

Click the button "Start Test" below to begin your visual acuity test.

When you are finished, sign "S" or click "Submit Test"
