Audio Response Vision Test

Welcome to the Ocular Vision Acuity Test for Audio-Response Users (can listen and respond verbally to commands)

Before you begin, please stand 3 ft away from the screen for a more accurate vision score

Place your device on a level surface where the chart on the screen is directly in your line of vision.

Close one eye and cover it with your palm. Your other eye will be tested for visual acuity.

Once you have finished testing that eye, you may switch eyes and repeat the test again for the other eye.

Click the button "Start Listening" below to begin speaking the letters you can see on the screen.

When you are finished, say "Stop" or click "Stop Listening"
